The Diocese of Pasig (Latin: Dioecesis Pasiginae) is one of the Dioceses of the Catholic Church in the Philippines that comprises the City of Pasig, Taguig (with the exemptions of the EMBO barangays (formerly part of Makati City),
which are under the ecclesiastical jurisdiction of the Archdiocese of Manila), and the Municipality of Pateros in Metro Manila, Philippines.

WHAT SERVICES CAN I REQUEST?

You can request the following services: - Baptism - Wedding - Funeral - Mass - Blessing Simply select the service you need, fill in the necessary details, and submit your request.

WHAT HAPPENS IF THERE IS AN ERROR OR ISSUE UPON PAYING ONLINE?

If you encounter any issues while making a payment, please send us a message through email pasiginae@gmail.com. If you still find trouble or doubts, WALK-IN's are still welcome! Just visit the Parish of your choice and settle your accounts!

HOW LONG DOES THE PAYMENT PROCESS WILL MAKE THROUGH THE SYSTEM?

Payment processing times may vary depending on the payment method used. Typically, payments are verified within 1-2 business days. Once the payment is confirmed, your request will be processed accordingly.
